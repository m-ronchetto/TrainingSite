What We Do

We are a free, confidential, identity-affirming, and volunteer-led helpline serving the St. Louis community. For any LGBTQIA+ needs, questions, or concerns, our helpline volunteers provide an empathetic listening ear.

Meet Our Team

Our team is made up of a passionate group of volunteers. As members of the queer community ourselves, we understand the isolation, stigmatization, and fear that arise from the lack of acceptance from people around us.
